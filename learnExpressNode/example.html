Eligibility Criteria

You can join the Scottish Widows GPP following your 3 months probationary period.

However, if you do not express your wish to join within your first 3 months, you may be automatically enrolled into the Company Workplace Pension Plan with The Peoples Pension.

Standard Retirement Age under the Pension Plan

65 years of age

Your Contribution

Members are required to pay a minimum of 2.00% of your basic annual salary. Your Employer will match your contribution up to a maximum of 5%.

The total amount of all pension contributions paid, (by you, and your employer) to this plan and to any other plan, must remain within limits set by HM Revenue & Customs.
